Hi!

Cambridge University Estonian Society (CUEST) has been created to gather Estonians in Cambridge as well as those interested in Estonia, its language and culture. Currently we have around 40 members.

The Society aims to give support to all new Estonians coming to study to Cambridge and act as a source of information for all those interested in studying in Cambridge.

We invite all Estonians studying and working in Cambridge to join us! If you have any questions, please contact us at
soc-cuest@lists.cam.ac.uk and join the Facebooki group CUEST.
